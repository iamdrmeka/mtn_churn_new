## MTN CUSTOMER CHURN: KEY INSIGHTS AND SUMMARY

### 1 - High Customer churn Rate

The analysis revealed an overall customer rate of 29.44%, indicating that nearly one-third od customers stopped Using MTN services which the observed period. This demands urgent attention as it leads to serious loss of revenue. Strategies for customer retention must be deployed immediately.

### 2- Regional Data

The analysis revealed very low patronage from the south east states, yet having the highest number of loss of customers from same region. **Analysis of the reason for churn and customer satisfaction shows that the average customer satisfaction around the region remains low and customers tended to skew towards competitors due to cheaper plans.** Efforts must be made to penetrate the market to understand the purchasing pattern, purchasing power and tailor out strategies to build on the market.

The North central recorded the highest number of customers and number 2 in churn at 33%; this is not right and posses significant risks if not resolved. The south shows a potential for serious growth as despite being number four in customer size, recorded the lowest percentage of customer churn (23%).

In Revenue generation: - The South east generated the least revenue. - The North cental generated the highest revenue - The South south despite being number five in customer base, went up to number three in revenue, indicating higher premium purchases from the region.

### 3- Gender Data

Although the customer base is evenly distributed with women being slightly more than men, the women generate about 52% of the total revenue which is higher than she female customer base percentage.

### 4- Age Demographics

- About 55% of the customer base are above 45 years old
- 29% are above sixty years old.
- Youths of 16-24 make up only 10% of the customer base.
- Young adults of 25-34 had the highest churn rate followed by the youths.

This data shows that the young population are less retained representing a major growth opportunity is we understand their purchasing habits and usage plans.

### 5 - Devices

The Data shows a wide range acceptance for the mobile sim card, this is reasonable as everyone at least owns a phone; however due to it's lower unit price, it generates the lowest revenue.

The 5g broadband device came number 3 on number of sales, however drove the highest revenue, indicating higher unit price as the volume of sales are almost the same with the other devices, but revenue gap is more than two times of what the other broadband devices generated.

### 6- Subscription Plans

The 1.5tb plan generated the highest revenue despite being among the bottom 5 plans.
The 60gb monthly plan sold the highest but dint make it to the top 5 revenue generators

This indicates: - Top selling products are not the most expensive. - Customers preferred Monthly plans due to affordability - A dive into what plans the top spenders purchases confirms that smaller to medium monthly plans were customer favorites.

### 7 - Major Reasons for churn

The top 5 leading case of customer churn - BEtter Offer from competitors - High call tariff - Poor Network - Costly Data plans - Poor Customer service.

This shows that affordability is the number one factor leading to customer churn followed by Poor service delivery.

### 8- Customer satisfaction

Customer satisfaction rate survey does not skew fully to the negative as approximately 61% of the customers gave a review of good to excellent.
Customer churn is mostly due to affordability and pricing and not majorly from poor customer service.
